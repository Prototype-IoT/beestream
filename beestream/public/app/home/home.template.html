What is Beemon?

The Beemon project began in 2012 to work to idenify the causes and signs of colony collapse disorder. Beemon, an abbreviation for Bee Monitoring, started with the goal of being a low-cost way to record video at the entrance of a honeybee hive. The project now includes video, audio, and temperature/humidity recordings at a honeybee hive all in a low-cost system. The project has also grown to include a number of analytics utilities that allow us to automatically count bee arrivals/departures at the honeybee hive, identify individual bees for analysis, and recognize oddities in audio files. With this information we hope to predict and eventually diagnose colony collapse disorder.
